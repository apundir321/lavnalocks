About Us

Welcome to Lavna Digital Locks, a destination that promises security solutions equipped with the latest technology. Established in 2008, the company is running under Bhagwan Shri Computers in India. Our locks have set a benchmark in renovating your place with a stylish touch while keeping up the high-quality safety standards. We are on a journey of equipping PAN India with keyless digital locks that will have the maximum level of security to ensure peace of mind. Everything that is locked is meant to be safe and Lavna locks have a futuristic management team who are browsing and working to make that happen. We at Lavna locks believe that one is enough. No other outsider can enter your safe place without your permission. Our locks are meant for people who believe that “it’s your home and only you should have access to your heaven”. You care about your assets, we care about your place.

Carpenter can easily install this lock Company will assist you if any problem occure and provide complete solution.

IS IT AUTOMATICALLY GET LOCK AFTER SOMETIME IF I FORGOT TO LOCK IT WHILE GOING OUTSIDE?

In a condition where you forget to lock, in that condition your home is safe Without fingerprint or pin or RFID card you can’t access your lavna digital locks.

DOES DOOR GETS LOCKED AFTER FEW SECONDS OR IT NEEDS TO BE MANUALLY LOCKED AFTER ENTERING HOME?

The lock consists of three units. the first unit lock automatically on shutting the door. the second has to be manually locked from inside and outside. the third is the do not disturb option.
